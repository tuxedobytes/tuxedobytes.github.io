First Post

Mar. 14, 2025

Finally got little time to make some progress on this side project.

This idea came to me years ago - Actually, I think the biggest nudge was when Valve introduced SteamDeck - I knew then that it is a great opportunity to introduce Linux to people who have very warped idea of what Linux and opensource sphere is.

I wanted to create a content channel related to Linux ecosystem and make tutorials targeted to complete novices and experienced users as well.

This project is still at the beginning (after all no Tutorial was yet published) but I have long TODO list which I want to go through.

My own journey to Linux, opensource, tech and programming also started from zero.

I think that I can share a valuable knowledge with you in the coming months/years and I hope you will find it worthy of your time.
